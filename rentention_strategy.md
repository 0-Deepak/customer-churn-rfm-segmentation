# Retention Strategy For Segments

### Champions
    Description :- very frequency and high monetary value customers, who engage with the platform regularly and make orders
    Strategy :- provide exclusive deals early access to the customers, make them feel special, deepening there loyalty
    Business Value :- Very High

### At-Risk
    Description :- Customers with Moderate Frequency and Moderate to high monetary worth, engage with teh platform but not very frequently
    Strategy :- Run campaigns and promotional offers for the, provide discount and deals in a regular timeframe or they may churn
    Business Value :- High
    
### Loyal
    Description :- Customer with high Frequency order and moderate to high spenders with decent engagement with teh platform, they are loyal customers and can be inducted into champion segment but also can slip into at risk segment
    Strategy :- Routine communication and product recommendation to them, induce them into loyalty program and give them community perks
    Business Value :- Very High

### Infrequent - Low Spenders
    Description :- Low frequency and low monetary value customers, they occasionally visit the platform and make small or one time purchases
    Strategy :- low cost campaign engagement for them, avoid expensive actions or major discounts as they may not turn into major business revenue source
    Business Value :- Low

### Discount Sensitive
    Description :- Customers how ony place order infrequently and only when discount is available, they tend to make order only when promotional deals are active 
    Strategy :- Keep them buying with giving heavy discounted deals, and lower the discount gradually, as sharp price hike may drive them away
    Business Value :- Medium

### Infrequent - High Spenders
    Description :- Customer how has low frequency but high monetary purchase behaviour, they may become a good revenue source for the business if converted to a regular high frequent user
    Strategy :- do predictive marketing for them tending to there needs, try to convert them into a regular customer
    Business Value :- Medium
    
### Recent Customer
    Description :- Customer with low frequency of orders, because them have signed up recently
    Strategy :- get them to a second and third purchase to quickly get them in a habit and move them to loyal or champion segment
    Business Value :- High - Medium

### Dormant
    Description :-  customers with low to no activity on the platform and have not made a purchase for quite some time
    Strategy :- Once in a while run a low-cost reactivation campaign with small budget, but the conversion and reactivation might not be very high
    Business Value :- Low

# Campaign Budget Prioritization
    If Campaign budget is limited, the priority of the customer segment will be
    Champion > Loyal > At Risk > Recent Customer > Discount Sensitive > Infrequent High Spender > Infrequency Low Spender > Dormant
